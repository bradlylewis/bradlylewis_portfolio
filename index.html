My Story

What draws me to software development is that it can literally take you anywhere. Software developers are needed in just about every industry. I also really like the people. Since it takes so long to become competent in development work, most of the people are extremely driven and thirsty for knowledge. Over the years, I've been exposed to all sorts of technologies; some more than others. Without a doubt, I'm most skilled with JavaScript. I've used it the longest because it's been the most accesible to me. I've gotten to work with React, Angular, Vue, Node.js, Ember.js, Three.js, and many more libraries. I've also done a bit of AI/ML work using Python. I've worked on Frontend, Backend, Web, Mobile, Gaming Consoles and Software. I love to learn so I'm always setting aside time to learn things that maybe I don't have the opportunity to utilize at work. During my studies, I've learned a bit about Java, C#, Linux, Scripting and more. Software development suits me because I like to create things, I'm good at problem-solving, I'm always trying to find ways to be more organized and I'm a people person.
